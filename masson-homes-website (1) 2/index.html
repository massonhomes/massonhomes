Need A Quick Close?

Skip the listing. Sell directly to us.

Not every home needs to go on the market. If you need a certain close date, can't or won't invest in prep and showings, or your property needs work we'd rather handle ourselves — we'll make a direct cash offer.

No photos. No showings. No repairs. No financing contingency. A firm written offer within 72 hours of walkthrough, closed in as little as 14 days on your timeline.

Makes sense if your home is…
  • In need of major repairs, renovation, or updates you don't want to handle
  • Inherited, vacant, or behind on taxes — with a messy title situation
  • On a tight timeline — divorce, relocation, pre-foreclosure, estate closing
  • Occupied by tenants you'd prefer not to coordinate around
  • A tear-down or land-value play better suited to a builder than a retail listing
