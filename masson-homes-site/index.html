The approach

A calmer way to sell — on purpose

Masson Homes was built around a simple idea: full-service representation should not cost a percentage of everything you have built. We set the fee up front, set expectations before we begin, and reserve our work for sellers who are ready to move.

Every listing is handled by a licensed agent, start to finish, on FMLS.
