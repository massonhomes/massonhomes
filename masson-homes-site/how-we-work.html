How we work

A clear process,
set out first

Expectations set before we begin — not halfway through.

Selling a home is a partnership. We agree on the plan, the price, and the fee before any work begins — so there are no surprises, and no one’s wheels are spinning.

The process

From first call to closing

01

Consultation

Fifteen minutes on your home, your timeline, and your goals. We confirm whether a flat-fee listing is the right fit before anything else.

02

Pricing analysis

A grounded look at what your home should list for, based on current comparable activity in your market. A pricing analysis, not an appraisal.

03

Prepare and stage

A practical plan to get the home ready to show — the work that earns the strongest first impression and the best offers.

04

Market

Professional photography, an FMLS listing with full syndication, and coordinated marketing built to reach the right buyers.

05

Negotiate

Offer review and skilled negotiation, with your equity as the priority through every counter and contingency.

06

Close

Transaction management from accepted offer to closing day, coordinated through Wynd Realty.
