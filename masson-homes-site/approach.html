The approach

A calmer way
to sell

Full-service representation, without a percentage of everything you have built.

Masson Homes started with a simple question: why should full service cost a percentage of your sale?

Built around your equity

The traditional model ties an agent’s pay to your price. We set a flat fee up front instead. The work is the same full-service work — pricing analysis, preparation, professional marketing, negotiation, and the close — but what you keep at the end is more of your own equity.

Set out before we begin

We agree on the plan, the price, and the fee before any work starts. That clarity is deliberate. It means no surprises, and it means we reserve our work for sellers who are ready to move.

Local, and licensed

We focus on Roswell, Alpharetta, Milton, and Sandy Springs, and serve the wider north-metro area. Every listing is handled by a licensed agent, start to finish, on FMLS, through Wynd Realty.
